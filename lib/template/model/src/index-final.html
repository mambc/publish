Amazonia Deforestation

This application shows the evolution of infrastructure, territorial units, and deforestation in the Brazilian Amazonia. It gathers data from different sources, including INPE, Ministry of Environment, National Water Agency, and IBGE. It is a Google Maps application with a left panel where you can select the data to be visualized an a bottom panel where you can manage time. This application is a contribution of the Brazilian National Institute of Science and Technology (INCT) for Climate Change funded by CNPq Grant Number 573797/2008-0 e FAPESP Grant Number 2008/57719-9.
